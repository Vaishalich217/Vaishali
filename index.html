THE BMW

*BMW, which stands for Bayerische Motoren Werke (Bavarian Motor Works), is a German multinational manufacturer of luxury vehicles and motorcycles, headquartered in Munich, Bavaria, Germany.

*BMW currently has 10 distinct series groupings which include 6 for cars and 4 for SUVs or EVs, encompassing models like the 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, X, Z, i, and M series.


Here's a breakdown of the BMW series
Sedans:

  • 1 Series: Compact sedan
  • 3 Series: Compact to mid-size sedan
  • 5 Series: Mid-size to executive sedan
  • 7 Series: Full-size luxury sedan
Coupes and Convertibles:
  • 2 Series: Compact coupe or convertible
  • 4 Series: Compact coupe or convertible
  • 6 Series: Mid-size coupe or convertible
  • 8 Series: Grand tourer coupe and convertible
  • Z Series: Roadsters
SUVs and EVs:
  • X Series: BMW's line of SUVs
  • i Series: BMW's electric vehicle lineup
  • M Series: High-performance variants of BMW models
